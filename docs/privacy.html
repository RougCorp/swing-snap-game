Privacy Policy — Swing & Snap

Last updated: April 10, 2026

RougCorp ("we", "us") operates the mobile game Swing & Snap (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

1. Information we collect

Swing & Snap does not require you to create an account and does not ask for any personal information such as name, email, phone number, or location.

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect:

  • Device information (device model, operating system, language)
  • Advertising identifiers (Google Advertising ID)
  • Approximate location derived from IP address
  • Interaction with advertisements

This data is collected by Google and used to display personalized or non-personalized advertisements, depending on your device settings.

2. How data is used

We do not collect, store, or sell any personal data ourselves. The only data collection happens through Google AdMob for advertising purposes. You can learn more at Google's Advertising Policy.

3. Local data

The App stores your progress (score, unlocked items, settings) locally on your device only. This data is never sent to any server.

4. Children's privacy

Swing & Snap is rated for everyone but is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Your rights

You can reset the advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads in your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads).

6. Contact

For any question about this policy, contact us at: rougcorp@gmail.com
